Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I work on a wide range of web development projects including websites, web applications, e-commerce platforms, and mobile-responsive solutions using modern technologies like React, Node.js, and more.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple websites can take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications may take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with remote collaboration using tools like Zoom, Slack, and project management platforms. I maintain clear communication throughout the project.

What is your development process?

I follow an agile approach: discovery & planning, design mockups, development with regular updates, testing, deployment, and ongoing support. You'll be involved throughout the process.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly. This includes updates, backups, and technical support.

How do you handle project pricing?

I provide custom quotes based on project scope, complexity, and timeline. I offer both fixed-price projects and hourly rates depending on your preference and project type.
